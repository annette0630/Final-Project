JONGHYUN

Revisiting the solo career of an artist who will forever be 27.

SOLO DISCOGRAPHY

After debuting in 2008 as a member of group, SHINee, Jonghyun made his official debut as a solo artist in 2015. His solo work showcases a very different musical style than that of SHINee, speaking to his talents as an individual artist as well as a songwriter and a composer.

BASE

Jan. 18, 2015

“BASE” was Jonghyun’s official debut as a solo artist. The album is comprised of 5 songs and features multiple big-name artists.
